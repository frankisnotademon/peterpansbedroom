Why I Love Folk Punk

-The lyrics! They're painfully honest in a way that most music isn't.

-There's like one guy that makes most of the music for the genre, so it's predictable. *cough* Pat the Bunny *cough*

-The instrumentation makes me think of being at Dollywood. I think I still miss tennesee.

-When it talks about self-destruction, it's about how it's bad and hopefully that's better for my brain to hear than depressing stuff without hope.
